Disaster Life Cycle: Be Ready, Stay Safe, Rebuild Stronger

Disasters affect lives in three critical phases — before, during, and after. Preparedness helps reduce risks, quick response saves lives in the moment, and recovery brings communities back to strength. Together, awareness, action, and unity can turn tragedy into resilience.
