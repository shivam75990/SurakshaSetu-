Immediate Actions

What to Do

  • Listen to local authorities and emergency broadcasters for instructions.
  • Stay with your family and do not split up.
  • Shelter in the safest part of your home, away from windows.
  • Shut off utilities if instructed or if you smell gas.
  • Conserve your phone's battery life.

What NOT to Do

  • Do not use your phone for non-essential calls. Texting is better.
  • Do not drive unless absolutely necessary or instructed to evacuate.
  • Do not go outside to sightsee or inspect damage.
  • Do not ignore official evacuation orders.
  • Do not drink tap water until it's confirmed safe.
